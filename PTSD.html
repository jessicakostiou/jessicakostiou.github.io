PTSD Web-Based Video Game

As a part of the Software Engineering degree at Monmouth University, it is required that student participate in a senior design course in which teams of three develop a piece of software and carry it through the full software development life cycle. My project is currently still in its early stages, however I am currently in contact with a non-profit organization called Invisible Wound who is interested in creating a mobile app or web-based game to take the player through the cognitive stages of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder. They plan to use this tool as a part of their sensitivity training for their corporate clients to help them understand the impact of PTSD. This final product will be presented at the Monmouth University Research Conference this coming spring. Stay tuned for more updates as we begin development!
