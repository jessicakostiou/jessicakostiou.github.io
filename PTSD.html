Battlemind

Currently developing a web based video game about the effects of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder on daily life for a non-profit organization. Battlemind is aimed to show the player how soldiers lives are effected after being discharged, and teach methods for helping their loved ones cope in specific situations. The organization plans to use this as a learning tool, especially for children of war veterans. The final product will be presented at the Monmouth University Research Conference this coming spring. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue development!
