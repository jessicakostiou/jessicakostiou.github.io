Course Registration System Requirements Document

Students were instructed to create a requirements document to build an alternative to Monmouth University’s course registration system. Students were broken into teams of four and were expected to collaborate on the components of the system and what features would need to be encompassed in the functional and non-functional requirements. Additional items such as data dictionaries, entity-relationship diagrams and use-case diagrams, were utilized in order to demonstrate the goal of the software being proposed.
