Don't Just Go Green, Grow Green.

Mission Statement:

At Growing Green Eatery our goal is to Provide high quality, healthy food for our customers, while being kind to the environment.

General Info:

We offer great tasting, low calorie food for our customers on a daily basis, no matter what meal you come for. All of our ingredients used in the preparation of our food are grown in a greenhouse attached to the restaurant. Guests are able to eat in the greenhouse (or inside the restaurant), eat delicious food, and enjoy live entertainment on weekends. Growing Green Eatery offers a unique dining experience which is both memorable, and healthy at the same time.
