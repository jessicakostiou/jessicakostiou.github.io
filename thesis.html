Honors Thesis: Human Computer Interaction

Currently involved in research with Allen Milewski, a Software Engineering professor at Monmouth University. This is a study based on "The Tweenbot Project" conducted at the Museum of Modern Art in NYC. It is to be used as Individuals will be observed to see if they are more inclined to help a robot who is more visually appealing and extroverted vs. a bland and introverted robot. Human interactions will be video recorded, compared and contrasted. This experiment will be accompanied by an in-depth analysis on Human Robot Interaction that can be read below.

This study will be occuring in Fall'2014 and will pe presented at the Monmouth Univesity Honors Research Conference in Spring '15.

Manifestation and Classification of Personality in Robots
